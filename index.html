Background

Briyoni grew up in Atlanta,Ga where she graduated high school, and continued her education at Savannah state university. As a student, Briyoni was active outside of her studies. She was an operations and marketing tutor to her peers. She competed and placed 2nd at the South East Decision Institute (SEDI) annual research paper competition, where she was recognized for her work on corporate social responsibility and how it relates to guilt appeals in marketing and Advertising.

Quickly after earning her B.B.A in Marketing(2015), she began working as a paid intern for a medical equipment supply for a year. The internship was turned into a full time position to keep Briyoni on the team.

Briyoni returned to Atlanta in 2017, where she became a local marketing assistant and office manager for a tech company located in Buckhead, Ga. She currently works for Code Crafters Academy as a Business/Marketing manager. Briyoni also works with Bester Foundation, a non-profit organization for homeless families, as a active board member and consultant for crowdfunding.
